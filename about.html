About Nexjone Square

Focused on Sports Infrastructure Development

Nexjone Square Private Limited specializes in sports infrastructure development, sports facility planning and project consultancy for synthetic hockey turfs, athletic tracks, football grounds, indoor courts, swimming pools, stadium lighting and multipurpose sports facilities.

Our Foundation

Built Around Sports Infrastructure, Engineering & Long-Term Facility Value

Nexjone Square Private Limited is positioned as a specialized sports infrastructure company focused on creating reliable, performance-driven and long-lasting sports facilities for schools, colleges, academies, stadiums, government bodies and private institutions.

The company’s work is centred around sports facility development including hockey astro turfs, synthetic athletic tracks, football and cricket grounds, badminton and squash courts, multipurpose indoor halls, swimming pools, stadium upgrades and technical project consultancy.

Sports-First Direction

Our direction is clear: to focus on sports infrastructure that supports healthier communities, stronger institutions and better training environments for athletes. Every project is planned with attention to surface quality, drainage, safety, durability, usability and future maintenance.
