Project Delivery Models

Flexible Sports Infrastructure Development Models

Nexjone Square Private Limited supports sports infrastructure projects through structured development models suitable for government bodies, institutions, schools, colleges, sports academies, private investors and public facility owners.

PPP Model

Public Private Partnership

We support sports infrastructure development under Public Private Partnership models where public institutions and private partners work together to create accessible, commercially viable and professionally managed sports facilities.

Suitable for schools, colleges, municipalities and public bodies
Shared development responsibility and long-term facility planning
Helps create public-access sports infrastructure with private expertise
Discuss PPP Opportunity
BOT Model

Build Operate Transfer

Under the Build Operate Transfer model, sports facilities can be developed, operated for a defined period and later transferred as per agreed project terms. This model is ideal for revenue-generating sports infrastructure and long-term asset creation.

Build sports facilities with planned operational structure
Operate the facility for usage, memberships, events or training
Transfer the asset after the agreed project period
Discuss BOT Project

These models can be explored for sports complexes, box cricket arenas, football turfs, pickleball courts, badminton courts, swimming pools, athletic tracks, multipurpose halls and other sports facility projects.
